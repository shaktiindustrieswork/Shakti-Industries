Regular Bench

A regular bench is a simple, sturdy seating structure designed to accommodate multiple people. Typically constructed from wood, metal, or a combination of both, it features a long, flat seat supported by stable legs. Some benches include a backrest for added comfort, while others maintain a minimalist, backless design. Commonly found in parks, gardens, public spaces, gyms, and indoor settings, a regular bench provides practical, durable seating suitable for rest, conversation, or everyday use.

Key Features

  • Sturdy steel frame construction.
  • High-quality, durable work surface.
  • Ergonomic height for comfortable use.
  • Easy to clean and maintain.
  • Available in multiple sizes.
  • Powder-coated finish for longevity.

Belt Conveyor Machine

A belt conveyor is a ubiquitous material handling system that moves items, bulk materials, or packages from one location to another using a continuous loop of material—the conveyor belt. This belt is stretched over a frame and is supported along its length by a series of rollers called idlers. The belt is driven at one end by a motorized pulley (the drive pulley), which provides the traction to move the belt and its load. The other end houses a return pulley that guides the belt back to the loading point.

Key Features

  • The primary advantage of a belt conveyor is its ability to provide a continuous and consistent flow of material.
  • Can be straight, inclined, declined, or even include curves. They can be horizontal or set at an angle to change elevation.
  • Durable construction for long service life.
  • Ranges from small, delicate conveyors in electronics assembly to massive, kilometer-long systems in mining.
  • Customizable to specific requirements.
  • Belt Material: Can be rubber, PVC, fabric, metal mesh, or plastic modular, chosen based on the product (e.g., food-grade, high-temperature, abrasive materials).
